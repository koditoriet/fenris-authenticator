Privacy policy

Unlike other apps and websites that promise to take user privacy very seriously, on the screen detailing the several hundred ad networks it shares your data with, Fenris does not collect any user information whatsoever, personal or otherwise. As we do not collect any information, we obviously have nothing to share with any third parties.

Google does collect technical information — such as hardware specifications, frequency of crashes, etc. — about all devices on which Fenris is installed through Google Play however, and shares an aggregate of this data with us. We receive no information about individual users.

If you would prefer to opt your device out of this dataset, we recommend that you install Fenris directly from our GitHub repository instead.
